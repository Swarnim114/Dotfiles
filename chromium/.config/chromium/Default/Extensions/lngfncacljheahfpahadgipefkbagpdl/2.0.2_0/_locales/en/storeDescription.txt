Experience true cinematic viewing on your ultra-wide (21:9) and super ultra-wide (32:9) monitor with UltraWide Video! This extension optimizes videos across your favorite sites—no more black bars.

Key Features:
 • Two display modes: Zoom (crop-in to fill) and Stretch (scale width) to match your preference.
 • Precise control: Smooth scale slider with presets for 16:9, 18:9, 21:9, and 32:9.
 • Apply outside fullscreen: Keep your preferred fit in windowed mode, not just fullscreen.
 • Works automatically on supported players; toggle and fine‑tune anytime from the popup.

Shortcuts (configurable):
 • Toggle mode (Zoom/Stretch):
     • Windows/Linux: Ctrl+Alt+C
     • macOS: Ctrl+Cmd+C
 • Increase/Decrease zoom:
     • Windows/Linux: Ctrl+Up / Ctrl+Down
     • macOS: Command+Shift+Up / Command+Shift+Down
 • Jump directly to aspect presets (16:9, 18:9, 21:9, 32:9).
 • Manage or change shortcuts at: chrome://extensions/shortcuts

Compatibility:
 • YouTube
 • Netflix
 • Max (HBO)
 • Prime Video
 • Disney+
 • and many more!
